Why Donate?

Water, forests, land are all in danger, save the environment, save the future.

Donation is needed to meet some of the financial expenses for gathering resources for their conservation and to accelerate the mission.

Yuva Paryavaran Sanrakshan Sena has been working in the interest of the environment. This army works for the protection of thousands of lakhs of forests, that is, the first goal of our army is to plant trees and take care of them. The slogan of all of us is to plant trees. Our goal is that with this verse we are taking our army forward and efforts are on to inspire others with this verse. To plant trees in the environment, I want you all to inspire each other towards the environment and do your best every month. Plant trees as per Wah Karave If you do not have time then please donate trees to our army so that we can plant trees. Support us in doing this noble work whether it is physically or financially “Plant Trees Save Lives” Our Yuva Paryavaran Sanrakshan is the main verse of Sena and there are many such verses with which we inspire people and always try to keep our Sena aware! To join this army, search A2ypss on our Facebook page and don't forget to like, follow and share.

Thank you!
